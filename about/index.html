About TUNU Publishers

"Elimu ni Tunu" — Celebrating educational excellence, cultural heritage, and critical thinking with curriculum-centered learning in Kenya.

Who We Are

Established in Kenya with a vision to revolutionize learning materials across the world, TUNU Publishers has grown to become a reliable, household publishing name in Kenya. The Swahili word "Tunu" translates to a rare gift, treasure, or a gem. We operate on the profound philosophy that "Elimu ni Tunu" (Education is a Treasure).

Our main mission centers around producing exceptionally curated materials aligned with the Competency Based Curriculum (CBC). From Junior Secondary revisions to culturally resonant stories, our materials ensure concepts are absorbed with clarity, critical thinking, and joy.

Whether you are a parent looking for imaginative grade-appropriate storybooks like Kosa la Popo, or a secondary student navigating critical studies with reference titles like Kudurusu Fasihi, we walk along with you at every stage of the academic journey.
